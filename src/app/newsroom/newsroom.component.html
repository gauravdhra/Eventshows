News

Sairyō is a start-up based in Ottawa, Canada with the mission of helping artists monetize their talents online through livestreamed events while also raising money for COVID-19 relief. We cater to a wide variety of artists including musicians, DJ’s, dancers, comedians, chefs and many more. We believe anyone with a talent or skill can be an artist.

Our event platform offers artists the opportunity to create free and paid online events. For all events, attendees have the option to donate to the World Health Organization’s COVID-19 Solidarity Fund. Additionally, artists can choose to donate a portion or all of their earnings towards the COVID-19 Solidarity Fund.

Sairyō has team members all over the world helping us achieve our mission including Canada, Australia, Colombia, India and Lebanon. With a global reach, we hope to provide plenty of much-needed entertainment and financial relief to as many artists and communities as we can.

Sairyō will be forever free for the artist. We do not take a cut of event earnings or donations and focus on helping others and raising funds for COVID-19 relief.
