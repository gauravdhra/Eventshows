About US

Sairyō re-defines online experiences. The platform financially supports artists and COVID-19 vaccine research through artist livestreams.

We know that 2020 has been difficult start. Sairyō was created as an innovative new way to stream live talent and get paid as if you were in a live ‘gig’. We cater to a wide variety of artists. Anyone from musicians, DJs, dancers, comedians to at-home artists (Tik Tok) and more. Anyone is an artist and has something to provide via livestream.
